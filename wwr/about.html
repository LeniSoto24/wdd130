Leni's Raft

About Us

Here at the Leni's Raft, our whitewater rafting trips designed to provide thrill-seekers with an unforgettable experience on the water. Our expert guides ensure safety while you navigate through stunning landscapes and challenging rapids. Come with your family and enjoy camping and rafting with us.

History

Leni's Raft was founded in 1994 by Lehi Pedraza during one of his aventures trips. While he was fishing, he fell into the river and was pushed by its current.

At first, it was terrible, but after a while, he enjoyed the situation and wanted to share that feeling with everyone. His experience has grown into a provider of whitewater memories.
